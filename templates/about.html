About BlockParty

BlockParty is a web app that provides users a way to buildup the community around them. It's for people who want to engage with neighbors that share similar interests, such as hiking, playing boardgames, discussing books, and coding. By entering their "homebase," the user can search for upcoming public activities and events within their vicinity using Meetup and Eventbrite APIs. Event details, including name, time, description, and link can be easily viewed geographically on a Google map or by date on a list. Users can login to save and view their favorite events, which is updated when events expire.
